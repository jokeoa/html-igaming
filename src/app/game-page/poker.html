About Poker

Poker is a legendary card game combining strategy, psychology, and luck. Build the best five-card hand using your cards and community cards. Master hand rankings from High Card to Royal Flush, and use betting rounds to outplay your opponents.

How to Play

  • Each player receives 2 private hole cards
  • Five community cards are dealt in stages
  • Bet, check, raise, or fold during each round
  • Make the best 5-card hand from 7 available cards
  • Win the pot with the strongest hand or bluff!
