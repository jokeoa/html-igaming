About Blackjack

Blackjack is one of the most popular casino card games. The goal is simple: get your hand value as close to 21 as possible without going over. Face cards are worth 10, Aces can be 1 or 11, and all other cards are worth their face value.

How to Play

  • You and the dealer each get 2 cards to start
  • Your cards are face up, dealer has one face down
  • Hit to take another card, Stand to keep your current total
  • If you go over 21, you bust and lose
  • Beat the dealer's hand without busting to win!
