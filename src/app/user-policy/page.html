User Policy

HTML Casino Educational Platform

Effective Date: September 23, 2025

Last Updated: September 23, 2025

1. Purpose and Scope

This User Policy governs your access to and use of the HTML Casino educational platform, a demonstration website developed by students at Astana IT University. This platform serves exclusively educational purposes and provides demonstration gameplay experiences without monetary transactions or real gambling activities.

2. Educational Nature and Disclaimer

HTML Casino operates as a purely educational and demonstration platform. The website does not facilitate actual gambling, does not accept real money wagers, and does not provide opportunities for monetary gains or losses. All gaming features are implemented for educational purposes to demonstrate web development technologies and user interface design principles.

Users acknowledge that this platform does not promote, encourage, or facilitate real gambling activities. The demonstration games available on this platform are designed to showcase technical implementation rather than provide authentic gambling experiences.

3. Acceptable Use Guidelines

Users are expected to utilize the platform in a manner consistent with its educational objectives. Access to the platform is provided for learning purposes, technical evaluation, and constructive feedback regarding web development implementation.

Users must not attempt to circumvent, modify, or exploit any technical features of the platform. Any attempts to manipulate the demonstration games or access unauthorized areas of the website are prohibited and may result in immediate access restrictions.

The platform should not be used as a substitute for or introduction to real gambling activities. Users seeking actual gambling experiences should utilize properly licensed and regulated gambling platforms appropriate for their jurisdiction.

4. Age Restrictions and Accessibility

While this platform does not involve real gambling, access is recommended for users aged 18 and above to maintain consistency with gambling-related content standards. Educational institutions and supervised learning environments may provide access to younger users for legitimate academic purposes.

Users accessing the platform for educational purposes should do so under appropriate supervision when required by their academic institution or local regulations.

5. Content and Intellectual Property

All content, design elements, and source code on the HTML Casino platform remain the intellectual property of the development team and Astana IT University. Users may view and interact with the platform content for educational purposes but may not reproduce, distribute, or commercially utilize any platform elements without explicit permission.

The demonstration games and interface elements are provided for educational evaluation and should not be replicated for commercial gambling platforms or real money gaming applications.

6. User Feedback and Contributions

The development team welcomes constructive feedback regarding technical implementation, user interface design, and educational value. Users are encouraged to submit suggestions and report technical issues through the designated issue tracking system.

All feedback and suggestions submitted by users become available for consideration by the development team. Users retain no proprietary rights over submitted feedback, which may be incorporated into future platform iterations without compensation or attribution requirements.

7. Privacy and Data Handling

The platform operates with minimal data collection practices consistent with its educational purpose. User interactions are logged solely for technical functionality and educational assessment purposes. No personal financial information is collected, stored, or processed through this platform.

Users should not provide any sensitive personal information, financial details, or confidential data through the platform interface. The demonstration nature of the platform does not require such information for any legitimate functionality.

8. Technical Limitations and Availability

Users acknowledge that HTML Casino operates as a student development project and may experience technical limitations, temporary unavailability, or functional restrictions. The platform is provided on an "as available" basis without guarantees of continuous operation or error-free performance.

The development team endeavors to maintain platform functionality during active development periods but cannot guarantee uninterrupted service or immediate resolution of technical issues.

9. Modification of Terms

This User Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in platform functionality, educational objectives, or regulatory requirements. Users are responsible for reviewing policy updates and continuing use of the platform constitutes acceptance of any modifications.

Significant policy changes will be communicated through appropriate channels available to the user community and academic supervisors when applicable.

10. Contact and Support

Questions regarding this User Policy, platform functionality, or educational applications should be directed to the development team through the official project repository or designated academic channels at Astana IT University.

Users experiencing technical difficulties or seeking clarification regarding acceptable use should utilize the established feedback mechanisms rather than attempting unauthorized access or modification of platform features.

This policy governs an educational demonstration platform and does not constitute terms for a commercial gambling service. All users must comply with applicable laws and regulations in their jurisdiction regarding access to gambling-related content, even in educational contexts.
